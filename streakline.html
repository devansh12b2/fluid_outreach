Streakline





If we focus on one particular section of fluid flow then after certian period of time we would have number of identifiable fluid particles in the flow, all of which had, at some time, passed through one fixed location in space. The line joining these fluid particles is defined as a streakline.

Equation of streakline

x=xinj+∫∨.dt

where xinj is position of injection point.

For Steady flow, streaklines, pathlines and streamlines all are identical.
