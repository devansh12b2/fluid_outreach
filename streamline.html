Streamlines





In a flow field the lines that are tangent to the direction of flow at every point in flow field are called Streamlines.Since the streamlines are tangent to the velocity vector at every point in the flow field, there can be no flow across a streamline. It is most comman technique of flow visualisation.

In a steady flow velocity at a point in flow field do not change with time, so streamlines shape do not vary from one instant to other; from this we can say that a particle moves along a single streamline.
