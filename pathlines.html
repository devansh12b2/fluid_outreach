Pathlines





If we observe a single particle via Langrangian approach(observing each particle individually) then the trajectory traced by that particle is called its pathline.

Equation of pathline

∨⃗=∨⃗(s,t)=ds/dt

The path of motion:-

⇒ ⃗s=∫∨⃗(s,t)dt

Unlike streamlines pathlines can cross each other.
